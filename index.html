bio 💊

Septia is a pharmacist who also dabbles in science writing and dataviz. Though the three knacks don’t seem to relate to each other, she always strives to showcase human narratives and put community front and center in her projects. Previously Septia wrote for Asian Scientist Magazine and Journal of Young Investigators. In her free time, she usually marvels at the beauty of the world through cycling 🚴🏻‍♀️, backpacking, exploring beaches 🌊 with extreme terrain, and snorkeling 🤿. For her sound travel advice and unfiltered thoughts, visit her Medium.
